Since 1853

For over 170 years, Ebenezer has been a place where people find faith, community, and purpose.

What started as a small Methodist church on one acre of donated land has grown into a vibrant community serving Milton and the surrounding areas. Through fires, rebuilding, and decades of change, one thing has remained constant: our commitment to sharing God's love.
