What We Believe

We believe in one God, revealed in three distinct persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit.

We believe in God the Father, the eternal, all powerful, all-knowing, ever-present Creator of all that exists.

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of Man and the Son of God, the promised Messiah and Savior of humanity.

We believe in the virgin birth of Jesus Christ, his sinless life, his crucifixion, his death for the sins of all humanity, and his resurrection.

We believe all people are born with a sin nature and are saved by grace, through faith, in the atoning work of Jesus Christ, who is the Way, the Truth and the Life.

We believe in the Holy Spirit, the Spirit of Truth, our indwelling Advocate sent by the Son and the Father.

We believe all professing Christians are part of the Church, the Body of Christ, and are gifted by the Holy Spirit.

We believe Christ will return in power to establish an eternal kingdom and to judge the living and the dead.

We believe we have been set free in Christ and are called to live an abundant, joyful life.

We believe we are to love one another and be the servants of all, as a testimony that we are disciples of our Lord Jesus Christ.

We believe in the resurrection of the body and life everlasting.

We believe the Bible is the inspired word of God, our ultimate authority, and is useful for teaching, equipping, and correcting.

We believe our mission is to make disciples of Jesus Christ for the Kingdom of God, and we do so in the name of Jesus Christ to the glory of God the Father.

Have questions? We'd love to talk.
