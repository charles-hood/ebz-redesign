Outreach

Serving our neighbors, sharing God's love

← Back to Connect

Our outreach ministry lives out our mission: "To know the love of God, grow as disciples of His son Jesus Christ, and go share His love with others." Through partnerships with local organizations, we serve families, veterans, seniors, and neighbors in need throughout North Fulton and beyond.

Food Assistance Programs

SouperBowl Food Drive

Each year, our annual canned food drive collects soup and stew donations, distributing thousands of meals through North Fulton Community Charities (NFCC) to families facing food insecurity.

SERV International Partnership

We provide nonperishable food boxes through our partnership with SERV International, ensuring food-insecure individuals and families have access to nutritious meals.

Disaster Relief

When disasters strike, Ebenezer serves as a donation site to support relief efforts. We've collected supplies for hurricane victims and other communities in crisis.

Community Support

Drake House

We support single mothers in the Drake House program with care packages containing personal items, gift certificates, and encouragement as they work toward stability and independence.

Cherokee County Homeless Veterans Program

Through donations and funding, we support homeless veterans in Cherokee County, helping provide housing assistance and essential resources.

Coat Drive

Our annual coat collection provides warmth to neighbors in need during the cold winter months.

Senior Engagement

Senior Services North Fulton

We donate puzzle and word game books to benefit senior citizens through Senior Services North Fulton programs, supporting mental engagement and community connection.
